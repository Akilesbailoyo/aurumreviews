## Keyword Strategy Framework
### Tier 1: Commercial Intent (money keywords)
- "best X for Y" / "top X 2026" / "X vs Y"
- High competition, high reward
- Need 50+ topical authority articles before targeting

### Tier 2: Informational Intent (trust builders)
- "how to X" / "what is X" / "X explained"
- Build E-E-A-T, get backlinks naturally
- Should be 40% of content mix

### Tier 3: Question Keywords (AEO gold)
- "is X worth it" / "does X work for Y" / "X or Y for Z"
- Perfect for FAQ sections and standalone articles
- These get cited by AI engines most often

## On-Page SEO Checklist
- Title: Primary keyword in first 60 chars
- Meta: Keyword + benefit + call to action in 155 chars
- H1: Matches title intent (not identical)
- First 100 words: Contains primary keyword naturally
- H2s: Secondary keywords, question-format when possible
- Images: Alt text with keyword context
- Internal links: 3-5 per article to related content
- External links: 2-3 to authoritative sources (builds trust)
- Word count: 1,200 minimum for commercial, 800 for informational

## AEO Optimization Protocol
To be cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI:
1. **Direct answer format**: "The best X is Y because [specific reason]"
2. **Structured data**: Use markdown tables, numbered lists, definition-style answers
3. **Specificity**: "charges in 47 minutes" beats "charges quickly"
4. **Recency signals**: Include current year, recent data, "as of [month year]"
5. **Consensus framing**: "According to [X] reviews analyzed..." 
6. **FAQ schema ready**: Every article ends with 5 Q&A pairs in clear format
